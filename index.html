First post - COVID-19 Lockdown

I am off work for 3 weeks due to COVID-19 outbreak so I decided to learn something. I've started coding like 3 times now but lack of time (and laziness) has stopped me from continuing so I'm hoping this time I will get a bit further ahead.
I try to keep a normal routine in lockdown - wake up at 7am get dressed etc, go for a walk most evenings for our allowed 1 hour of outside exercise. Go shopping once a week. And learning this to make good use of my time but also not to be bored or run out of things to do.
So far I've learned about all kinds of tags, values and properties but I keep forgetting what is what. Tags are the easiest. I just need to use their proper names more.
